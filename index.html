History

Established in 1985, Harbour College has grown into one of the region’s most respected higher education institutions. With a commitment to academic excellence, research innovation, and ethical leadership, the college has produced thousands of successful alumni serving globally.

Vision

To become a globally recognized institution fostering innovation, integrity, research excellence and social responsibility.

Mission

To provide transformative education, promote entrepreneurship, and empower students with knowledge and skills for a dynamic world.
