Undergraduate Programs

BCA

Software Engineering, AI foundations, Cybersecurity basics and Cloud Computing.

Curriculum highlights
  • Programming in Python & Java
  • Data Structures & Algorithms
  • Introduction to Databases & Web Development
  • Foundations of Artificial Intelligence

Duration: 3 years • Eligibility: 10+2 (any stream) with maths/computer preferred

BSc Computer Science

In-depth theory and applied computing—ideal for research and advanced technical roles.

Curriculum highlights
  • Discrete Mathematics & Theory of Computation
  • Operating Systems & Computer Networks
  • Machine Learning & Data Analytics
  • Software Engineering Project

Duration: 3 years • Eligibility: 10+2 with science/maths

BCom

Commerce with modern electives such as Business Analytics and E-commerce.

Curriculum highlights
  • Accounting & Financial Reporting
  • Business Statistics & Analytics
  • Taxation and Corporate Law
  • Entrepreneurship & Start-up Lab

Duration: 3 years • Eligibility: 10+2 (commerce/science/arts)

Postgraduate Programs
